Pizza

Description

The most famous and iconic dish in Italy! Pizza! Everyone loves it and everyone has surely tried at least once to make one for themselves but without great success.

But today with this recipe you`ll be able to recreate a pizza worthy of a starred restaurant!

Ingredients:

For the Dough:
    Flour Water Yeast Patience
For the Topping:
  • Tomato sauce
  • Oregano
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • A leaf of basil

Steps:

  1. Knead the dough and wait a couple of hours.
  2. Spread the dough on an oven tray.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce and add the desired quantity of toppings.
  4. Slam it into the oven.
