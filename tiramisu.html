Tiramisù

Description

What type of rubbish site would it be without a dessert in its fine list of delicacies? The Tiramisu is an orgasmic type of dessert for any type of caffein addict with a sweet tooth craving.

It is perhaps the hardest dish of the bunch but it surely won`t disappoint!

Ingredients:

  • Savoiardi biscuits
  • Mascarpone cream
  • Caffè
  • Cacao

Steps:

  1. Brew some coffee and cool it to room temperature
  2. Dip the Savoiardi and lay them in an orderly fashion in a baking tray
  3. After completing a whole layer, spread Mascarpone cream as evenly as possible
  4. Repeat the process until you reach the desired height
  5. Let it rest in the fridge for a couple of hours
  6. Enjoy!
